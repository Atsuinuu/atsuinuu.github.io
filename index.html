The Artist

I'm a digital artist specializing in character design and visual storytelling. With over a decade of experience in the animation and games industry, I bring characters to life through nuanced design and painterly technique.

My work spans production illustration, concept art, and independent comics — always chasing the intersection of strong silhouette, expressive color, and narrative clarity.

Currently open for freelance commissions, production contracts, and collaborations on passion projects.
